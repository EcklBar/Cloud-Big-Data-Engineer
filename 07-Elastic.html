1. [cite_start]Inputs [cite: 1133]

Ingests data from various sources. Common plugins include:

  • beats (from Filebeat/Metricbeat)

  • http, syslog, kafka, jdbc

Intermediary processing. The most famous is Grok.

[cite_start]

MATCH: %{IP:client} %{WORD:method} %{URIPATHPARAM:request}
3. [cite_start]Outputs [cite: 1160]

Where to send the data. An event can be sent to multiple outputs.

  • elasticsearch (most common)
  • file, email, slack, s3
